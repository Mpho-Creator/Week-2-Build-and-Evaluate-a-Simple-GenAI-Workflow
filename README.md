# Dorm Decor Inquiry Responder

## Business Workflow

This project automates the first response to customer inquiries for a dorm interior decorating business.

## Who is the user?
The user is the business owner — someone running a small dorm decorating service who receives inquiries from college students interested in getting their dorm room styled.

## What input does the system receive?
A customer inquiry message describing the student's style preferences, budget, dorm size, and any specific requests or questions about the service.

## What output does the system produce?
A warm, personalized response email that acknowledges the customer's preferences, gives a brief overview of how the service works, and invites them to book a consultation.

## Why is this valuable to automate?
Responding to every inquiry individually takes time, especially during busy move-in seasons. A consistent, personalized first response ensures no inquiry goes unanswered, keeps the brand voice professional, and frees up the owner to focus on the actual decorating work. Human review is still recommended before sending.
